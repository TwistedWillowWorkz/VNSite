What is Vellichor?

❦

Vellichor is the strange, wistful nostalgia you feel when browsing a used bookstore. It is a deep, pensive awareness of the passage of time, evoked by the scent of aging paper, drifting dust motes, and the realization that every old book holds a life and story you'll never fully know.

"The sacred lifeblood of old pages."

The Origins of the Name

The word is poetically blended from vellum, the parchment upon which stories were once preserved, and ichor, the sacred golden lifeblood of the gods in Greek mythology.

Together they evoke the idea of stories as living things—timeless, treasured, and worthy of being passed from one reader to the next.
