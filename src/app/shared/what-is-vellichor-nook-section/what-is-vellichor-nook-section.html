A reading home in the making

What is Vellichor Nook?

Vellichor Nook is a cozy digital companion for readers — a place to track your books, treasure your reading journey, and eventually share the adventure with fellow book lovers.

📚

Track Your Reading

Keep your personal library organized with books you want to read, are currently reading, and have finished.

✨

Treasure Your Journey

Celebrate your progress through ratings, reviews, reading stats, challenges, and reflections.

🌿

Share the Adventure

Future community features will help readers connect through friends, book clubs, shared favorites, and cozy conversations.
